About Us

Evergreen Tradesource is a wholesale exporter and importer of Tree nuts based out of New Jersey, USA. Our focus is to source branded goods from the Americas at low and competitive prices to satisfy the ever increasing demand worldwide. We carry a wide range of both food and non-food products. We specialize in full line groceries, confectionery, and non-food items with consistent availability, exceptional lead times and maximum product shelf life.We’re able to supply over 20,000 products including Nestle, Kraft, P&G, Unilever, Pepsi, General Mills and many, many more.

Our expertise

Hudson has the expertise to prepare products for seamless export to the Middle East and Asia, and boasts modern in-house date coding and labeling facilities.
